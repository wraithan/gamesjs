PDX Games.js

When?
Sept 14th 2013, 9am to 5pm, RSVP here
Where?
Mozilla PDX: 1120 NW Couch ST, #320
What?
PDX Games.js is a hackathon for all those who are working on games in JS. This could be browser games, NodeJS games, or any other game powered primarily by JS. We will hack until there is 1 hour left and then we will do presentations.
What should I bring?
  • Laptop (or any other hacking device)
  • A project (either one in mind or an existing one)
  • Yourself
Food/Drink?
Mozilla is sponsoring food and drink.
Who?
Wraithan is organizing it. email/tweet at him if you have any questions. Also you can find him in #pdxnode channel on chat.freenode.net
Site source available on GitHub.
